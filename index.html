About Us

Where Bold Stories Come to Life

Duke Vepruar Theatre is a brand new independent theater company built on the belief that the stage is a place for raw, unflinching storytelling. We are founded on a passion for original works that challenge, move, and inspire.

We champion new voices and bold narratives — stories that dare to be different. Our productions are intimate, immersive, and fiercely original. We are currently building our debut season and cannot wait to share what we are creating.

“The stage is not a refuge — it is a battleground for the soul.”

Founder & Artistic Director

Ardi Ajeti

Visionary • Playwright • Director

Ardi Ajeti is the founding force behind Duke Vepruar Theatre. With a deep love for the transformative power of live performance, Ardi founded this company to create a home for bold, original works that push the boundaries of independent theatre.

A writer and director at heart, Ardi believes that theatre should be visceral, honest, and unapologetically human — a space where stories that matter are given the stage they deserve.

— Ardi Ajeti
